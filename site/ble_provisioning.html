home recovery github

WiFi setup.

Send your WiFi credentials to a Kiri Bridge over Bluetooth. The device joins the network and the rest of setup happens on its local web UI. Chrome or Edge required, 2.4 GHz only.

Bluetooth checking Device not selected WiFi not configured
01

Look for blue.

A fast blue blink means the Kiri Bridge is advertising over BLE and ready to be configured. If you don't see it, power-cycle the device and wait a few seconds.

02

Pick the device.

Choose the BLE device named PROV_KIRI_…. Keep this tab open until WiFi setup finishes.

03

Send WiFi.

The password stays visible — easier to catch typos before you send. 2.4 GHz networks only.

04

Open the portal.

When WiFi connects, the device's local web UI is reachable on your network. That's where you upload the firmware and pair with HomeKit.
