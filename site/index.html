CN105 to HomeKit

Make your Mitsubishi heat pump feel native.

Kiri Bridge is a small ESP32 controller for Mitsubishi CN105 indoor units. Plug it in, join WiFi, pair with Apple Home, and keep control local.

Control

Heat pump commands in Apple Home.

Power, mode, target temperature, and fan control map from Mitsubishi CN105 into HomeKit and the local WebUI.

Setup

No app store detour.

Use the browser to provision WiFi over Bluetooth. The device portal runs locally at port 8080.

Recovery

Firmware you can bring back.

If something goes sideways, the browser flasher restores Kiri packages over USB serial.

Getting started

For most buyers, setup is one page.

Power the device, wait for the blue blink, open WiFi setup, then pair it with Apple Home from the device portal.
